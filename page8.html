Organizational Structure of Lovers Foundation

The General Assembly consists of RM, AM1 & AM2 type of members.

The Board of Directors consists of RM type members, excluding those RM type members, who are part of Governing Council.

The President is the head of the NGO, followed by the VP, who is also the ex-officio Speaker of the Assembly. 

The CEO is the head of the Governing Council, followed by the Administrative Manager.
