WHAT OUR PRO SAYS

On Education System- Disguised Scholarships

(Warning: Dead Grammar Ahead)

New Scholarships launched for higher studies, more spoon-feeding for Ladakhis, still not happening what should.
Such imbroglios and we are fooled easily. These corrupt elite are also helping the other outsider bureaucrats and businessmen. Our corrupt & inward-looking, will never mend the rotten higher education in the region.
This will make us to migrate to Jammu, Delhi etc. Many people get benefited from these things, for instance;
As the number of migrating student grows, a considerable mass migration will be highlighted subconsciously in the public and concerns will be highlighted at a bureaucratic level, again subconsciously in the public. As common men as parents or guardians have to sacrifice a lot from their pockets, the rotten eggs (chavinists) will cheery pick solutions. They will pick the solution which are in the best of their and their sinister friends' interest.
How?

A good looking and beneficial solution for them is asking governments for scholarships for students migrating outside. We all know that in the subcontinent, corruption is an integral part of governmental systems. As we are well aware, scholarship scams are something you can realize by just a bit of thinking and intuition. So the cherry picked scholarship solution is firstly beneficial for the corrupt bureaucrats and thus, politicians.
Now the other part of such cherry picked solution is that there won't be any measures taken to strengthen higher education in our region in the first place. This however seems to be the most obvious solution but no, they will never do this. One reason has been stated and the other possible reason is more shocking.
As we all know again, the number of Ladakhi students in places like Jammu are increasing year by year. Thousands of students are in Jammu today.

The least estimation would be around 2000 minimum (maybe the number is 3 or 4 times larger).
Let's assume these 2000 students are living in 900 apartments. And let's assume there are 500 houses housing the apartments. So are literally giving away on average Rs. 60,000 to one house annually. I have no problem with others making money, what I am concerned is about a single couple paying around Rs. 30,000 as their kid's room rent per annum. Do remember that this 30,000 is only the room rent on a single couple per annum.
This shows how we are being looted.
Now the other more possible part is the "EDUCATION INDUSTRY", mostly tuition centres. There is not much needed to show how we throw our money on tuition centres. Big Education Industrialists benefit a lot from our migration and suffering.

We, only the common people can spend so much on migration and other expenses on such a massive and shocking level. I ask you people why cannot we donate even the 4th amount for our higher education in our region and not let our youth migrate? Thus strengthening the system?
As, Mr Khadim Hussain (President @Lovers Foundation) once raised a possibility in an article, that the migration of primary school students will lead to erosion of basic educational background. Tell you what, this is already happening. For instance, nowadays rich parents are often seen sending their kids mostly, and most probably the still lower class majority will follow this trend of the higher ones. This will prove catastrophic. As the wound deepens, harder it will be to heal.
Unfortunately, massive awareness campaign is needed for such obvious things.

It is the materialism and selfishness of ours that we are not able to see the reality, the simple solutions and respective executions.
Wake up people, cuz all these are blatantly happening, and our representatives have betrayed us, the only uncertain part is whether they are conspiring or not. Sounds a bit exaggerated but things are really looking bad. It is high time that we start working for our land.

Tufail Ahmad Khalu
 Public Relations Officer
